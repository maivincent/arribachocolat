Mestizo - Dark Gourmet Chocolate - 85% Premium

20 May 2024

This is a chocolate with surprisingly restrained flavor, not helped by the delicate physical nature of the bar. By letting it melt slowly in the mouth, one can extract slightly bitter notes of walnut and rind, followed by subtle caramel and a small touch of acidity.

Herencia Esmeraldas - 70% Dark Chocolate with Banana

12 May 2024

Of high quality, this fragrant chocolate fills the mouth with a thousand sensations. Herencia’s rich cocoa — woody, fruity, and influenced by cane sugar — combines with the round, salty crunch of dried bananas.

Arariwa - Chocolate 72% with Pineapple

10 May 2024

Why aren’t there more pineapple chocolates? Arariwa offers an excellent treat here. The candied pineapple pieces under the bar add their flavor and acidity to the chocolate’s roundness. You don’t taste fine cacao nuances, but it’s very pleasant and goes quickly!

Wiñak - Organic 70% Cacao

25 Apr 2024

This is the second Wiñak bar I’ve tried, and it’s once again an extraordinary chocolate. It’s fairly gentle yet complex, herbal like a fine black tea, with comforting notes of cream and pecan, and touches of salted caramel and malt. It lingers long in the mouth, unfolding a symphony of flavors. A magnificent chocolate, thanks to the Kichwa of the Río Napo.

Mazorca Negra - 70% Origin Tuzantán

14 Apr 2024

Another Mexican chocolate, found at Mango y Chile in Bacalar. This bar uses cacao from Tuzantán in Chiapas. The bar is made from criollo (lizard-tail variety according to the box).
