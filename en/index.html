Salapi - 70% - Harvest 2025

31 Jan 2026

On the box, Salapi specifies the origin of the 2025 harvest: 56% from Los Rios, 44% from the Galapagos.

Rights - Chocolate with a Purpose - 70% Aji

31 Jan 2026

Rights is the brand whose mission (and marketing strategy) is to employ people with disabilities. Since my last experiences with this brand, the box design has evolved: from festive colors to a more refined image.

Kallari - Sacha - 71% Hampik Ushay with Ginger

18 Jan 2026

Kallari’s cacao is, as always, very gentle. Here it is accompanied by candied ginger pieces and ginger essence present in the chocolate itself. The result is a calm, composed chocolate that doesn’t aim to overwhelm with intense or singular flavors. It is rather a small, fragrant, and pleasant sweetness.

Muyus - Otavalo - 60% Passion Fruit

17 Jan 2026

In Otavalo, north of Quito, the Muyus café sells coffee and chocolate. They produce their own bars, though the small print reveals the bar was made by Huma — a collaboration.

Lua - 85% Ancestral Ecuadorian Cocoa

16 Jan 2026

Another chocolate from Lua, all in little touches, as if painted by a pointillist artist. Very gentle — give it time to reveal itself, and the delicate flavor touches emerge one by one.
