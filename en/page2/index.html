Conexion - 75% Fine Cacao - Los Rios

15 Jan 2026

Fruity and floral, as the purple box of this Conexion chocolate states. It’s true: a light mix of bitterness and acidity recalls grapefruit, rounded by the sweetness of red grapes and powerful fermentation-derived notes.

Napo Cultural Center - Yasuni Pure Bar

05 Jan 2026

I found this bar in the souvenir shop of the Napo Cultural Center, located in Yasuni National Park, within the Kichwa Añangu community deep in the Ecuadorian Amazon. In a thatched hut, tucked among beautiful handmade jewelry by indigenous women, a small foil-wrapped package.

Chicao - 100% - Fine Aroma Cocoa

01 Sep 2025

Green mint, fir, lemon, bread and butter. The tasting of this 100% chocolate goes through different stages as you let it melt in the mouth or as you eat several pieces. It’s a pleasant chocolate, not bitter at all; on the contrary, it is rich, satisfying, and is enjoyed as you discover its nuances.
