This is not a flavored chocolate in the strict sense. And yet, the ingredient list includes machica (toasted barley flour) and cedrón — two discreet presences that make it quietly unique.

Gentle and enveloping, it evokes a chestnut cream with vanilla, both in taste and texture. The toasted barley brings warm cereal notes, while the cedrón adds a touch of herbal freshness.

A fine, original, and quietly comforting chocolate, in Huma's tradition of excellence
.
P.S. The packaging, illustrated with Tigua mask artwork, deserves a mention of its own — as if the artisanal care within is reflected on the outside as well.
