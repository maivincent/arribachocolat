Light, pure, almost ethereal. Notes of vanilla, caramel and bitter almond. And a hint of that cocoa flavor that reassures us, just when we were beginning to doubt: yes, this is chocolate.

A unique bar, that bets on finesse and difference at the expense, perhaps, of the comfort we seek in chocolate.

### Evaluation

_Uniqueness_: 5  
_Finesse_: 4.5  
_Comfort_: 4  
_Intensity_: 4  
_General impression_: 4.5  

**Final evaluation**: 22/25
