Another chocolate from Lua, all in pointillist touches — very gentle, give it time to reveal itself, and the delicate flavor notes emerge one by one.

Opening with wood and bark, it evolves toward peppermint and honey, finishing with a surprising hint of cola.

I repeat: it's very, very gentle. But let the velvety squares melt slowly, and fine subtleties will find you.
