> Note: Raw bars tend to present a more vegetal and astringent profile.

Pacari's 90% raw bar is unapologetically intense. It opens with bold roasted notes, then shifts toward astringent dark fruit and an almost green vegetal edge. The texture is dense and long-lasting on the palate. This is a bar for those who seek extreme cocoa intensity.
