Rights is a brand whose mission — and marketing strategy — is to employ people with disabilities. Since my last experience with them, the packaging has evolved: from festive colors toward a more refined aesthetic.

The chocolate itself hasn't changed. Woody and elegant, it is lightly sweetened with panela. The chili — ají — is not very strong, remaining discreet in the background while bringing a pleasant warming touch.

### Evaluation

_Originality_: 4  
_Fineness_: 4  
_Comforting_: 5  
_Intensity_: 4.5  
_Overall impression_: 4.5

**Final evaluation**: 22/25
