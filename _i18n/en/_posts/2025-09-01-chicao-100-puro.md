Green mint, pine, lemon, bread and butter. The tasting of this 100% chocolate unfolds in stages — different notes emerging depending on whether you let it melt slowly or take several pieces at once.

It is a pleasant chocolate, surprisingly free of bitterness — rich, satisfying, and rewarding to those who take the time to follow its nuances.

Tasting this alongside the Chicao 70% — or simply re-reading that note from January 2022 — reveals how profoundly sugar reshapes the notes cocoa is capable of playing.
