Avanaa offers us a chocolate of great intensity. Right from the start, it unfurls strong notes of wild berries, sandalwood and even red wine. Although the cocoa comes from the Dominican Republic, I immediately thought of Alluvia's Vietnamese chocolate - they're in the same range. A successful chocolate from a Montreal bean-to-bar producer

### Evaluation
_Uniqueness_: 4.5  
_Finesse_: 4.5  
_Comfort_: 4  
_Intensity_: 5  
_General impression_: 4.5  

**Final evaluation**: 22.5/25
